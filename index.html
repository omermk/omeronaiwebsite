About

Hi, I'm Omer Mushahid Khan.

I've spent nearly two decades helping organizations adopt emerging technologies, leading transformation initiatives across consulting, cloud platforms, and global technology companies.

Most of the AI programs I've seen up close share the same failure pattern: the pilot works, the demo impresses the room, and six months later the organization operates exactly as it did before. The technology was never the blocker.

Today I focus on one question:

"How do organizations move from AI experiments to sustainable competitive advantage?"

Everything I write explores that question: from strategy and governance to implementation and organizational change, for the people actually responsible for making it work, not just the people signing off on it.

Insights based on experience with Google, AWS, major technology consulting and data companies. All views are my own.
